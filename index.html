ABOUT EVENT

Indian Korean music fest 2024

The Song Festival has been to promote cultural exchange among Asian countries. Over time, it has evolved into a prominent music festival in Asia, playing a central role in promoting cultural exchange throughout the region.

Just as different colors blend to create a rainbow, artists from diverse Asian countries have used music to embrace each other’s cultural richness. Now, the Asia Song Festival is ready to open the door to global cultural exchange, reaching beyond Asia.

We sincerely welcome all of you to the ‘Indian Korean music fest 2024’.

EVENT DETAILS

Date - 14-16 June

Host - Korea Singers Association. World Culture Interchange Association

Venue - Noida City Expo, UP Province, India
